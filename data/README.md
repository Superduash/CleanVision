# Training Data

Place your training images here:

- `clean/` — Images of clean hospital rooms (label: 0)
- `dirty/` — Images of dirty hospital rooms (label: 1)

## Guidelines

- Use JPG or PNG format
- Aim for at least 100+ images per class for decent results
- Images should be representative of actual hospital room conditions
- Variety in lighting, angles, and room types improves generalization

## Usage

Upload these images to Google Drive and use the `colab_training/train.ipynb` notebook to train the model.
